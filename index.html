WINDSTRIKE

a computer vision sand shooter

HOW TO PLAY
01Point your index finger at the camera
02Move your hand towards the camera to blow sand forward
03Pinch fingers together to focus the beam for more damage
04Don't let enemies reach you — they deal damage on contact
ENEMY TYPES
(>_<) SMALL — fast, low HP
(ಠ_ಠ) NORMAL — balanced
(ಠ_ಠ) BIG — slow, high HP
(°▽°) DODGER — strafes side to side
(▶▶▶) RUSHER — accelerates at you
(#_#) SHIELDED — focus sand to break shield
